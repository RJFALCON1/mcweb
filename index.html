OVERVIEW OVERVIEW OVERVIEW

Minecraft is a sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios. The game was created by Markus "Notch" Persson in the Java programming language. Following several early private testing versions, it was first made public in May 2009 before being fully released in November 2011, with Notch stepping down and Jens "Jeb" Bergensten taking over development. Minecraft is the best-selling video game of all time, with over 238 million copies sold and nearly 140 million monthly active players as of 2021, and has been ported to several platforms.

HOW TO PLAY HOW TO PLAY HOW TO PLAY

Minecraft is a game that allows players to build, explore, and create their own virtual worlds. To start playing, players must first download the game onto their computer or gaming device. Once the game is installed, players can create a new world or join an existing one. The game is played in first-person view and players are able to move around the world using the arrow keys or WASD. The main objective of the game is to gather resources, such as wood and stone, to build structures and create tools. Players can also explore the world, fight off monsters, and discover new areas. As players progress through the game, they can unlock new items and abilities to help them survive and thrive in the world of Minecraft.

Ender Chest

5/5 - The Ender Chest is a block in Minecraft that allows players to store items in a shared inventory accessible from any location. It is considered "amazing" because it eliminates the need for multiple chests in different locations and allows for easy access to items from anywhere in the game. It also has the added benefit of being unbreakable, making it a reliable storage option for players.

Warden

1/5 - The Warden is a new hostile mob that is added in Minecraft's version 1.17, it is a hostile mob that spawns in the deep dark, it's unique because it can't see the player if they are standing still but it can detect if the player is moving. Some players consider the Warden to be "bad" because it has high health, deals high damage, and can be difficult to defeat, especially when encountered in large numbers. Additionally, its ability to detect movement can make it difficult for players to evade it and its spawning in the Deep Dark makes it hard to fight back with the limited light source.
